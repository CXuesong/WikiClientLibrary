Remarks

Depending on the real-time changes happening on the server, the enumerated sequence from EnumItemsAsync may duplicate between each pagination, usually the first and/or the last 1 or 2 items. The result of EnumPagesAsync, however, has already been removed of duplicates.

For full-text search on Wikia, use LocalWikiSearchList.
