Remarks

The returned sequence will have the SAME order as specified in revisionIds.

The WikiPage of returned Revision will be a valid object. However, its LastRevision and Content will corresponds to the lastest revision fetched in this invocation, and pages with the same title share the same reference.

If there's invalid revision id in revisionIds, an ArgumentException will be thrown while enumerating.
